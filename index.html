How a detail actually moves — start to finish.

01
Book your window
Pick a package and a time. We'll confirm scope, vehicle size, and any trouble spots — so the estimate you get is the estimate you pay.
02
Hand-wash & decontamination
Exterior work starts: foam pre-soak, two-bucket hand wash, wheels and trim worked separately. On mid-tier and up, a full clay bar pass lifts embedded contaminants the wash can't.
03
Protection & enhancement
Sealant, wax, or paint enhancement goes on depending on tier. The goal isn't just shine — it's a protective layer that holds for weeks, not a day.
04
Interior deep-clean
Vacuum, extraction shampoo on seats and carpet, steam where applicable, leather conditioning, and a full wipe-down of every panel, vent, and seam.
05
Final inspection & handoff
We walk the vehicle with you — inside and out — before handing back the keys. If something isn't right, we fix it before you leave.
